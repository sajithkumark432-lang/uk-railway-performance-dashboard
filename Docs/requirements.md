# Requirement Document

## Objective
Provide leadership with a single dashboard to monitor UK railway performance
and identify growth opportunities.

## Key Questions
- Which ticket types and classes generate the highest revenue?
- What are the busiest routes and departure stations?
- What percentage of journeys are delayed or cancelled?
- What are the primary reasons for service delays?
- How does travel demand vary by departure time and ticket type?
- Which operational improvements can reduce delays and improve reliability?
  
## KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Revenue by Ticket Class and Ticket Type
- Total Number of Journeys
- On-Time, Delayed, and Cancelled Journey Rates
- Busiest Routes and Departure Stations
- Delays by Reason
- Journey Volume by Departure Time
